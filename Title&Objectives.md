Title and Objectives

title: SUTURA: A Web Based Tailoring Shop Tracker System

Objectives
To centralize and digitize tailoring business operations by providing integrated tools for
tier-based shop management, secure measurement storage, real-time order tracking, automated
notifications, and business analytics, ultimately improving both the tailor's workflow and the
customer's experience.

Specifically, this study aims to:
•Administrative Dashboard for subscription monitoring, the system
administrator will manage tailoring shop registration approvals, validate apparel
categories, verify branch map locations, and oversee platform-wide activity and
system performance.
• Subscription and Account Management for tiered plan enrollment,
the
tailoring shop will register on the system, select from Basic, Pro, and Premium
subscription plans, manage role-based staff access, and maintain active
subscription validity for continued platform visibility.
• Shop Discovery and Search Module for garment-based filtering, the customer
will search for tailoring services by specific garment type, filter results by
location and specialization, and view complete profiles of all registered tailoring
shops within Davao City.
• Map-Based Interface for location-based shop visibility, the customer will view
the branch locations of all registered and verified tailoring shops within Davao
City, filter shops by area, and access directions to selected tailoring shop
branches.
• Tailoring Shop Dashboard for storefront management, the tailoring shop will
configure their digital profile, manage service catalogs and apparel
specializations, set itemized pricing, handle appointment schedules, and control
their public platform visibility.
• Order Tracking and Measurement Module for real-time production
monitoring, the tailoring staff will record and retrieve digital customer
measurements and update garment production stages, while customers monitor
their order progress from placement to pickup.
• Interactive Analytics Dashboard for data-driven reporting, the system
administrator and tailoring shop owners will generate real-time visual reports on
subscription activity, monthly sales revenue, outstanding customer balances, and
individual shop productivity.
