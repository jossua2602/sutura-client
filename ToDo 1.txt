### PART 1: RESEARCH & BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE (The Foundation)
1. The "No DP, No Layout, No Cut" Rule: 50% Downpayment is strictly required before any production starts. 
2. Suki Culture: Loyalty is tracked via memory/saved records, not points. Saved measurements and corporate/family account histories are critical.
3. Hybrid Revenue Streams: Shops do Bespoke MTO (high margin), Bulk Uniforms (high volume), Rentals (with refundable deposits), and Used/Pre-loved Liquidation (selling off old rental stock).
4. Bulk Order Matrix: Team orders require a Player Name | Number | Size grid. Sizes are often standardized (S-3XL), not bespoke measurements.
5. Fulfillment Reality: Walk-ins and Store Pickups are dominant. Shipping/Local Delivery exists but MUST NOT integrate 3rd-party courier APIs. Status changes are manual.
6. Specializations vs. Services vs. Catalog: 
   - Specializations = Shop Identity & Searchability (e.g., "Bulk Uniform Expert", "Wedding Gown Specialist"). MOQ belongs here.
   - Services = The Actual Labor/Work (e.g., "Full Sublimation", "Hemming", "Bespoke Tailoring"). Prices here are optional ("Custom Quote").
   - Catalog = Physical Premade/RTW/Rental Items (e.g., "Andrea & Leo Gown", "Premade NBA Jersey"). Rent vs. Sale prices are separated here.
