## D. CUSTOMER ORDER TRACKING WORKFLOW (The Core Problem)

### Current Manual Flow (What Customers Experience Now):

```
1. Customer inquires via Facebook PM or walk-in
       ↓
2. Owner quotes verbally (no formal receipt or digital record)
       ↓
3. Customer visits shop for sukatan (measurement)
       ↓
4. 50% downpayment collected (cash or GCash)
       ↓
5. Order details written on a PHYSICAL JOB TICKET CARD
       (Customer Name, Garment Type, Measurements, Due Date)
       ↓
6. Card is pinned to the fabric bundle
       ↓
7. Customer goes home — NO UPDATES until due date
       ↓
8. Customer messages: "Sa na po ba?"
   Owner replies: "On going pa po." (Vague)
       ↓
9. Customer physically returns on pickup date
       ↓
10. Final fitting / claiming — balance paid before release
```

### Customer Pain Points Identified:
1. **No real-time visibility** — Customer does not know if fabric has been cut, sewn, or is in QC
2. **Vague responses** — "On going lang po" does not tell the customer anything specific
3. **No estimated pickup date communicated formally** — verbally given only
4. **No notification when ready for fitting** — Customer has to keep checking
5. **No proof of progress** — No photos sent during production
6. **Lost job tickets** — Physical cards get misplaced, especially during peak season
7. **Double appointments** — Customer arrives but garment isn't ready yet
8. **Peak season chaos** — Sotera explicitly says, "If we could finish every uniform in one sitting, we would! But we're humans too, we get tired."

## D. CUSTOMER ORDER TRACKING WORKFLOW

### Current Reality (The "Messenger Bounce"):
```
1. Customer sends Pinterest/TikTok screenshot via FB Messenger
       ↓
2. Owner quotes based on mental price matrix (fabric + complexity + qty)
       ↓
3. Customer sends 50% DP via GCash (screenshot as proof)
       ↓
4. Owner verifies GCash reference number
       ↓
5. Layout artist creates digital mockup → sent via Messenger
       ↓
6. Customer must explicitly say "Approved" or send screenshot
       ↓
7. Fabric cut in stacks using pattern
       ↓
8. Sublimation printer prints panels
       ↓
9. Heat press bonds dye to fabric
       ↓
10. Cut panels sent to row of sewers (mananahi)
       ↓
11. Sewing assembly (slower than printing — bottleneck)
       ↓
12. QC check
       ↓
13. Photo sent to customer via Messenger ("Order Released Today" post)
       ↓
14. Balance payment via GCash before shipping
       ↓
15. Shipped via J&T / LBC / Bus Cargo
```

### Customer Pain Points:
1. **Layout bottleneck** — Artists take too long, delaying printing
2. **Fabric shortages** — Running out of color mid-bulk-order
3. **Sewing bottleneck** — Can print 100 jerseys/day but only sew 30
4. **Fake GCash screenshots** — Common scam
5. **Color fading/cracking** — Previous suppliers delivered poor quality
6. **Sizing issues** — "Siguraduhing tama ang sukat!"
7. **Incomplete uniforms** — "Kulang-kulang, Hindi komportable"
8. **No real-time progress** — Customer just waits blindly

# 🎯 UNIFIED CUSTOMER TRACKING EXPECTATION (All Three Businesses)

Regardless of business type, **every customer wants to answer these 3 questions at any time:**

### 1. **"ANO ANG GINATAHI?" (What is being sewn?)**
- What garment type?
- What fabric?
- What size/measurements?
- What design/logo/name?
- How many pieces?
- What stage is it in RIGHT NOW?

### 2. **"SAAN NA ANG ORDER KO?" (Where is my order now?)**
- Which production stage?
- Who is working on it?
- Is it ahead of schedule or delayed?
- When is the next milestone?
- When is the estimated pickup/delivery date?

### 3. **"MAGKANO NA ANG NABAYAD KO AT MAGKANO PA?" (Payment status?)**
- How much DP paid?
- How much balance remaining?
- When is balance due?
- What payment methods accepted?

### FINAL RECOMMENDATION

A **unified tracking system** (like SUTURA) must support **all three business types** with:
- **Role-based dashboards** (Admin, Shop Owner, Staff, Customer)
- **Multi-stage production tracking** with real-time SMS/email notifications
- **Saved measurement repositories** per customer profile
- **Appointment scheduling** for sukatan and fittings
- **Payment tracking** with DP/balance logic
- **Map-based shop discovery** for customers
- **Rental lifecycle management** (for Fashion Shops)
- **Bulk team builder matrix** (for Sublimation Shops)
- **Printable QR-coded job tickets** (for all workshops)
- **Analytics dashboards** for shop owners and admins

This ensures that whether a customer orders a **school uniform from Sotera**, a **basketball jersey set from MAX**, or a **debut gown from a Fashion Atelier**, they can **always see what's being sewn, where their order is, and when they can claim it** — eliminating the "Sabay-sabay na naman ang claiman" chaos forever. 🎯
